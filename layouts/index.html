What to include in your request

To help make sure we are on the samge page and smooth the process, please include the following in your request:

  • Your contact.
  • Device model. For example, iPhone 6, or Galaxy S9. If you cannot tell the model, try the following:
    - If your device stills turns on, on iOS, you can go to Settings - General - Model Name;
    - On most Android systems, it's Settings - About - Model Name.
    - If your device doesn't turn on, and you cannot tell the model, you can attach a photo of the device.
  • Issue description. For example, device not turning on, or no charging.
